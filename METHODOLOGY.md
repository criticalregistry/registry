# Methodology

*Work in progress — full methodology to be published with v0.1.*

Risk scores combine four base criteria:

1. **Ecosystem reach** — how broadly deployed is the component
2. **Bus factor** — how many people must be unavailable to halt the project
3. **Language risk** — C/C++ codebases with shrinking developer pipelines score higher
4. **Funding status** — unfunded or episodically funded projects score higher

An OT presence multiplier of ×1.5 is applied where the component is confirmed embedded in operational technology environments (automotive, medical, rail, industrial).

Source data layers: OpenSSF Criticality Score (algorithmic baseline), SBOM disclosures, maintainer research (human intelligence layer).
